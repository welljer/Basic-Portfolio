About Me

New to Utah, moved here from Ohio about five years ago. We have been adventuring around the state ever since be it rock hounding out in the western desert, hiking to see petroglyphs, or mountain biking around the Wasatch Nation Forest.

We also dabble in art forms such as ink drawlings, origami, and dancing with poi. We appricate these forms due to the amount of detail that one has to have to excel at these art forms.

We have worked in retail for nine years and quickly moved acrossed deparments learning as much as possible. As well as have worked in customer service and billing

We got interested in web development due to the unique concept of using design languages to speak with web pages and having them interact with us and other users.
